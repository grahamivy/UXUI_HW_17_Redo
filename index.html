About Me

I am an American and British UXUI designer here to interrupt monotony and bring clarity into the day-to-day.
I believe our digital experiences should always be joyful and easy. I aim to produce intuitive, immersive designs by integrating innovation and elegance into modular design patterns. I want to push the boundaries of web design while delighting users and serving company needs.

My Background

An eclectic blend of the creative and the human. I have a degree in Political Science (another in Spanish) and experience working in startups such as Halara Corp.
Currently based in Minneapolis, Minnesota, I have also spent extensive time traveling, working and learning in Central and South America.
When I'm not designing, you can find me painting, hiking, listening to cumbia, cooking pasta, dancing samba, or pressing flowers.
To catch my attention, I'm passionate about design, climate justice, the outdoors, typography, food systems, and global history.

My Toolset

Webflow, Figma, ProtoPie, Sketch, Adobe XD, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign, Github, Visual Studio Code and Procreate.
